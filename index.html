Get to know me!

Hi! I am Maria Leah Maraviles a student who is pursuing Marketing Management at the University of the Visayas. I am a former President of the Rotaract Club of Cebu Fuente - University of the Visayas Chapter and currently the Vice President - Marketing External. With the use of this website, I'm excited to share with you my journey in service.

Coming from a humble background, I strived for academic excellence, determined to build a successful future. Yet, my ambition extended beyond myself; I craved a way to help others. That's what led me to join a fantastic organization in my first year of college. It provided the perfect platform to serve my community without straining my finances. From 2019 onwards, the experience of giving back was truly enriching. While my studies currently demand a lot of my time, I hold onto the hope of returning to service someday. contact me here.
